Our Services
After a long and strenuous week, let your body rest while you're pampered at Chic.

Essential Nail Care

From trims and polishes for your hands and feet to full package services with exfoliating scrubs and aroma therapy massage. Our most popular service is the mani-pedi combo.

Artificial Nail Enhancement

Pamper yourself with a fresh set of nails. We specialize in Acrylic, Bio-Gel, and UV Powder Gel nail sets.

Shellac

Say goodbye to chips, smudges and long drying times! This revolutionary product applies like nail polish and wears flawlessly for 14 days. While damage resistant and dries nearly instantly, the birth of Shellac is a miracle to us all.

Hair Removal

Discover the silky smooth surfaces of your skin through our hair removal services. We specialize in laser hair removal, as well as waxing, from head to toe. We have specially trained technicians with boasting reputations of their ability to gracefully zap away those stubborn hairs! We welcome both the ladies and the gentlemen!

Microdermabrasion

A great treatment to freshen up and revive dull skin! Diamond tip microdermabrasion is a mechanical exfoliation of the skin to remove the top layer of dead skin cells. Benefits include softening of fine lines and wrinkles, improve skin tone and texture, reduce the appearance of superficial hyperpigmentation, and enhances cell regeneration.
